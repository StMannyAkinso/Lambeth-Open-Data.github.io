MONITORING OUR PROGRESS

Tracking Lambeth’s Climate Action Plan progress helps us understand where we’re making positive strides, what is working, and where we need to accelerate action.

Lambeth’s Climate Partnership is tracking progress at three levels:

NET-ZERO TARGET

Metric: Overall reduction in emissions

Annual monitoring on the borough emissions inventory, tracking trends across sectors, and per capita emissions.

GOALS

Metric: Progress on Goals in Lambeth's Climate Action Plan

Tracking progress against the 20 goals and 5 climate themes – where trend data is available and possible to visualise.

ACTION

Metric: Progress on Actions in Lambeth's Climate Action Plan

Monitoring actions taken towards the goals, publishing an assessment of progress, insights, and barriers.
